# Street Art App

Street Art App is an Android mobile application designed to help users discover, document, and share street art in their local area. The app allows users to upload photos of graffiti, murals, stickers, posters, and other temporary urban artwork before they are removed, covered, or damaged.

This public repository is a project overview only. The source code is private.

## Overview

Street Art App was created as a college project to support the discovery and preservation of temporary street art. Street art can disappear quickly because it may be painted over, removed, weathered, or damaged. This app gives users a way to document artwork by uploading images, adding details, and connecting posts to map locations.

The project combines mobile app development, Firebase services, user authentication, image storage, metadata handling, and map-based discovery.

## Problem

Street art is often temporary and difficult to track. Many murals, stickers, graffiti pieces, and posters are removed or damaged before people have a chance to see them.

This project was built to create a simple digital archive where users can document street art and help others discover artwork by location.

## Data and Business Analytics Relevance

Although this project is a mobile application, it also connects to data organization and location-based analysis. Each uploaded artwork post can include structured information such as image data, user details, metadata, and geographic location.

This type of data could be used to analyze patterns such as:

* Areas with the most uploaded street art
* Types of street art users document most often
* User contribution activity
* Popular locations for urban artwork
* Changes in documented street art over time

These insights could support community art projects, local discovery, urban culture research, or location-based reporting.
